after rain

A lo-fi hip hop style piece created with electronic elements including synths and automations. Emphasis on low frequency through EQ, filters, and reverb. Made with FL Studio; audio visualizer rendered from www.renderforest.com.

Connection

Electronic dance music in the happy trance genre created with a variety of synths, bass, and more. Emphasis on heavy mixing & mastering (due to wide range of frequencies) and dynamics. Made with FL Studio; audio visualizer rendered from www.renderforest.com.

my nature of music

Final project for my Audio Foundation Studios course. Consists of three electronic music productions of distinct styles each complimented by videos of nature. Emphasis on a wide range of elements of sound design. Music made with FL Studio; video edited with Adobe Premiere.

Sound Design in Film

Assignment for my Audio Foundation Studios course. Emphasis on sound design for a muted short film using audio elements such as foley, EQ, panning, reverb, distortion, compression, and spatialization. Video edited with Adobe Premiere.
