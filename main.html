Jeehun Jimmy Hwang

I am a developer, creative, musician, and dreamer.

As a graduating senior of New York University's Tandon School of Engineering, I aspire to apply the skills that I have developed over the course of these years to inspire and leave a mark on people.

While I am concentrating on front-end / web development, my experience in graphic design has allowed me to expand my perspective on visual aspects which has strengthened my overall development as a creative. Furthermore, my passion for sound design (especially music production — a hobby that I've cherished since my beginnings), drives me to masterfully blend in the visual and audio elements of design which I hope to accomplish in my Senior Project.

I realize that there is still much room to grow and many more learnings to be done especially due to the fast-changing nature of technology, and this only brings me motivation to constantly make myself the best version of me every day.

My dream is to create a positive impact in this world through my work and to provide to my audience (you!) a token of my appreciation for spending their time wondering at my creations.

Audio

after rain

A lo-fi hip hop style piece created with electronic elements including synths and automations. Emphasis on low frequency through EQ, filters, and reverb. Made with FL Studio; audio visualizer rendered from www.renderforest.com.

Connection

Electronic dance music in the happy trance genre created with a variety of synths, bass, and more. Emphasis on heavy mixing & mastering (due to wide range of frequencies) and dynamics. Made with FL Studio; audio visualizer rendered from www.renderforest.com.

my nature of music

Final project for my Audio Foundation Studios course. Consists of three electronic music productions of distinct styles each complimented by videos of nature. Emphasis on a wide range of elements of sound design. Music made with FL Studio; video edited with Adobe Premiere.

Sound Design in Film

Assignment for my Audio Foundation Studios course. Emphasis on sound design for a muted short film using audio elements such as foley, EQ, panning, reverb, distortion, compression, and spatialization. Video edited with Adobe Premiere.
